The Shocking Link between Hidden Hunger and Heart Health…

And it could be plaguing your kids

Hidden Hunger is a type of malnutrition. It’s due to deficiencies in micronutrients, fiber, and more - which are essential to the health of your gut. And gut health is crucial to overall health.

But that’s not all. The NIH reports that a lack of micronutrients and fiber is also a threat to your heart health. For example, low levels of the vitamin E are linked to heart damage and low vitamin D to an increased risk of heart attack.

In short, if left unaddressed, Hidden Hunger could lead to heart problems later in life.
